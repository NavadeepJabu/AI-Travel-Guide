**3-Day Goa Itinerary**

**Day 1**

**Morning:** 
Begin the day with a 6:00 AM wake-up call and a delicious Goan breakfast. Head to Vagator Beach (approximately 1-hour drive), one of the most scenic beaches in North Goa. Enjoy the tranquility and breathtaking sunrise views from this stunning beach.

**Afternoon:** 
Post breakfast, proceed to Baga Beach (approximately 30-minute drive) for an adrenaline-packed day. Take a boat ride to explore the vibrant marine life of the Arabian Sea, and if you're feeling adventurous, try snorkeling or scuba diving to witness the beauty of Goa's underwater world. Enjoy a leisurely lunch at one of the beach shacks.

**Evening:** 
In the evening, head to the famous Tito's Lane (approximately 10-minute walk from Baga Beach) to experience the vibrant nightlife of Goa. This bustling stretch is lined with bars, clubs, and restaurants that pulsate with music and energy. Savor local cuisine and relish the lively atmosphere, or enjoy a tranquil massage at one of the nearby spas to unwind.

**Day 2**

**Morning:** 
Drive to the Dudhsagar Waterfalls (approximately 2-hour drive) for an early morning trek. Trekking the lush forests will take you to the enchanting waterfalls, which cascade into a scenic pool. The refreshing spray and stunning views will invigorate you for the next part of your day.

**Afternoon:** 
After the trek, proceed to the Old Goa area (approximately 1-hour drive), a UNESCO World Heritage site. Visit the magnificent Basilica of Bom Jesus (Sé Cathedral), and the Se Cathedral, to appreciate the grandeur of Portuguese colonial architecture.

**Evening:** 
Explore the quaint town of Panjim (approximately 30-minute drive), the capital of Goa. Stroll through the historic Fontainhas neighborhood, admiring the beautiful, colorful houses and vibrant street art. Enjoy dinner at a local restaurant, sampling the flavors of Goan cuisine, before heading back to your hotel for a well-deserved rest.

**Day 3**

**Morning:** 
Drive to the picturesque village of Ashwem (approximately 45-minute drive), known for its beautiful beaches and scenic views. Take a leisurely walk along the unspoiled Ashwem Beach, surrounded by serene palms and tropical flora.

**Afternoon:** 
Visit the iconic Palolem Beach (approximately 1-hour drive), famous for its tranquil and picturesque views. Take a boat ride or paddleboard along the pristine waters of the Palolem River, surrounded by lush mangrove forests.

**Evening:** 
As the day comes to an end, return to the bustling Baga Beach (approximately 30-minute drive) for a final evening of shopping and dining. Pick up some local handicrafts or souvenirs and enjoy one last Goan dinner at one of the many restaurants and bars along the beach before departing for the airport.
